Reimagining Education

The Next Education Workforce

ASU is redesigning the traditional "one teacher, one classroom" model to address teacher burnout and improve student learning.

Traditional Model

The Isolated Educator

  • Solo teacher managing 25-30 students
  • Expected to be an expert in all areas
  • High isolation and elevated burnout rates
  • One-size-fits-all lesson plans
VS
Next Education Workforce™

Team-Based Staffing

  • Collaborative teams of educators
  • Distributed expertise tailored to needs
  • Sustainable roles with peer support
  • More personalized student attention
